# 📊 Multivariate Analysis of the AI Job Market (2025–2026)

🚀 A data-driven project analyzing global AI job trends using multivariate statistical techniques to uncover key drivers of salary, demand, and job classification.

---

## 📌 Overview
This project explores a dataset of 1,500 AI job postings across 14 countries. The goal is to understand the underlying structure of the AI job market and identify factors influencing salary and demand.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Identify key drivers of salary variation  
- Reduce data dimensionality  
- Discover latent factors in job characteristics  
- Classify job roles into salary tiers  

---

## 📂 Dataset
- Source: Kaggle (AI Jobs Market 2025–2026)  
- Observations: 1,500  
- Variables: 25  
- Includes salary, experience, demand, benefits, and job features  

---

## ⚙️ Methods Used

### 🔹 Principal Component Analysis (PCA)
- Reduced dimensionality  
- Retained ~86% variance using 5 components  

### 🔹 Factor Analysis (FA)
- Identified 3 latent factors:
  - Compensation  
  - Market Demand  
  - AI Salary Premium  

### 🔹 Discriminant Analysis (LDA & QDA)
- Classified salary tiers  
- Achieved ~94% accuracy  

---

## 📊 Key Insights
- Compensation and demand are the main drivers of AI job trends  
- AI salary premium forms a distinct factor  
- Job categories are clearly separable using numerical features
